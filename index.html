FAQ

Straight answers

What is EchoFlow Voice?

It is an Android audio app for DAF, FAF, voice monitoring, audio tuning, and signal visualization.

Do I need wired headphones?

Yes. Wired headphones are recommended for a more stable and lower-latency listening path.

Does the app upload voice data?

No. In the current version, microphone audio is used locally for the app's core audio features.
