Features

Everything you need
to hear yourself clearly

Two dedicated screens for DAF and FAF, plus precise audio controls to shape your voice feedback exactly how you want it.

⏱

DAF — Delayed Auditory Feedback

Hear your voice with an adjustable delay from 0 to 300 ms. Three quick presets: Gentle (70 ms), Standard (120 ms), Strong (170 ms).

🎵

FAF — Frequency Altered Feedback

Pitch-shift your voice feedback in real time. Choose from presets or adjust pitch and mix manually.

🎙

Live monitor

Hear yourself with no added effect — low latency, real-time audio monitoring through wired headphones.

🎛

3-band EQ

Separate microphone and headphone equalizers — Low (120 Hz), Mid (1 kHz), High (6 kHz) — for full tone control.

📊

Audio visualizer

Real-time waveform display with multiple layouts and switchable colors. See your audio level at a glance.

🔇

Noise gate & de-esser

Clean up your signal automatically. Noise gate cuts background noise, de-esser tames harsh S and SH sounds.

🔒

Settings lock

Lock your DAF and FAF settings to avoid accidental changes during a session.

⚡

Latency calibration

Built-in calibration test fine-tunes the DAF delay to your specific device for the most accurate feedback.

🌍

English & Polish

Full interface available in English and Polish.

How to use

Up and running in seconds

No account, no setup, no subscription.

1

Connect wired headphones

Use 3.5mm jack or USB-C headphones. Bluetooth is not supported — too much delay for real-time feedback.

2

Open DAF or FAF

Choose the mode you want to use. Each screen has its own independent settings.

3

Tap to activate

Tap the status card to start. You will immediately hear your voice with the selected effect.

4

Adjust and explore

Use the sliders and presets to find the settings that work best for you. Hold "Speak without effect" to bypass the effect at any time.

Requirements

What you need

🎧

Wired headphones

3.5mm jack or USB-C. Bluetooth headphones are not supported due to high audio latency.

📱

Android 8.0 or higher

Works on most modern Android phones and tablets.

🎤

Microphone permission

Required to capture your voice for real-time audio processing.

🔇

Quiet environment recommended

For the clearest feedback, use the app in a reasonably quiet space.
