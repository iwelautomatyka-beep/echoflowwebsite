DAF

Delayed auditory feedback

DAF plays your voice back with a short delay in wired headphones. This changes how you hear your own speech and lets you compare different delay settings.

The result depends on the phone, the headphones, microphone level, and audio settings, so small tuning steps matter.

FAF

Frequency altered feedback

FAF changes the pitch of the monitored voice without adding the classic DAF delay. It gives you a second listening mode on the same setup.

Comparing DAF and FAF side by side makes it easier to hear which mode feels clearer and more comfortable on your device.

Quick setup

How to get the best result

  1. Connect wired headphones.
  2. Turn on DAF or FAF.
  3. Adjust delay or pitch first.
  4. Then fine-tune loudness, filters, and microphone behavior.

Good to know

EchoFlow Voice works best in a quiet place with the phone microphone and wired headphones.

FAQ

What is EchoFlow Voice?

It is an Android audio app for DAF and FAF, voice monitoring, audio tuning, and signal visualization.

Do I need wired headphones?

Yes, wired headphones are recommended for stable sound and lower delay.

Does the app record or upload my voice?

In the current version, the app uses the microphone locally for audio features and does not upload voice data to its own servers.

Is this a medical app?

No. EchoFlow Voice is an audio tool focused on DAF, FAF, monitoring, and audio setup.
